Welcome to Moon Viewer!

You can control aspects of the scene with the controls on the right as well as click and drag to orbit around the model.

This website uses both a color map and a displacement map from NASA's official site.

In order to get a better look at the geometry of the moon, you can increase/decrease the displacement scale on the right. However, extending too much can lead to visual artifacts. You can also enable wireframe mode for a clearer look at the vertices of the model. Mess around with the other aspects of the scene, like the lighting and color.
